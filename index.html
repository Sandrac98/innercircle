Welcome to Inner Circle, a place where k-pop fans can be themselves.

Inner Circle was founded in 2015 by a group of K-Pop fans who wanted to create a space where they could enjoy their favorite music without having to worry about harassment or bullying.


Since then, we have grown into a community of over 30,000 members from all over United Kingdom who want to spend their time relaxing and meeting new friends. We are very proud of our community and hope that you will become part of it! We have so many meetings going on at the same time, so everyone can join in.


Inner Circle is also a great way to find your favorite k-pop idol's merchandise that you can't find anywhere else! We are always looking for new ways to improve our events so please drop us an email if there's anything we can do better!
