Welcome to Inner Circle
A place where k-pop fans can be themselves.

Inner Circle was founded in 2015 by a group of K-Pop fans who were passionate about creating a safe and welcoming space for other fans to enjoy their favorite music. Since then, the organization has grown significantly, and now includes a community of over 30,000 members from all over the United Kingdom. Our members share a common love for K-Pop and a desire to spend their time relaxing and making new friends with others who share their interests. We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone can feel comfortable and enjoy the music and culture that they love.
